PEC - III: Any course from any cluster is fine 
PEC - IV: Since the students are studying “24UIT611Cloud Computing” course in VII semester, it is better    
                    to choose a course from   Clusters B, C or D

* It is better to choose Courses from a Cluster in the prescribed order
